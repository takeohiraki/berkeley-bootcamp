How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Buy ingredients, including bread, peanut butter, and jam
  2. Obtain a butter knife
  3. Get two pieces of bread and put it on a cutting board
  4. Use the knife to scoop 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  5. Spread the peanut butter on one side of the bread
  6. Use the knife to scoop 1 tablespoon of jam
  7. Spread the jam on the other side of bread
  8. Put the two pieces of bread together so that the jam and peanut butter are touching
